Research

What we do

DECO Lab pioneers research across the full computing continuum — from embedded AI on microcontrollers to cloud-scale distributed systems — with a commitment to sustainability and ethical data practices.

LLM & SLM Integration

Leveraging large and small language models for intelligent system automation, architectural recovery, and documentation — with built-in compliance for the EU AI Act and ISO standards.

Sustainable Cloud Engineering

High-performance cloud tools designed for energy efficiency and long-term sustainability.

TinyML & Embedded AI

Machine learning models that run on resource-constrained IoT devices — enabling intelligence at the edge.

Data-Driven Resource Balancing

AI offloading strategies and adaptive resource allocation across heterogeneous computing environments.

Efficient MLOps

Data-aware ML pipelines and operations that reduce overhead and improve model lifecycle management.

AI for Sustainability

Applying AI and software engineering methods to tackle climate and environmental challenges.

Ethical Data Ecosystems

Compliant data spaces, knowledge-graph management, and trustworthy data sharing frameworks.

Industry Collaboration

Partnering with companies to cut compute costs and translate research into real-world impact.

Education & Training

Advancing Danish distributed-computing education through curriculum development and student involvement.
